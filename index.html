WELCOME TO I3CSET-2026

The 2nd International Conference on Computing, Communication and Sustainable Energy Technologies (I3CSET-2026) will be held on 30 September – 01 October 2026 at Galgotias College of Engineering and Technology (GCET), Greater Noida, India.

View on Google Maps


I3CSET-2026 provides a premier platform for researchers, academicians, and industry professionals to present and discuss recent advances in computing, communication, and sustainable energy technologies, fostering collaboration and innovation aligned with the conference themes.
