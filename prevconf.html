2024: International Conference on Communication, Computing, and Energy Efficiency Technologies (I3CEET 2024)

International Conference on Communication, Computing, and Energy Efficiency Technologies

The International Conference on Communication, Computing, and Energy Efficiency Technologies (I3CEET) 2024, held from September 20-21, 2024, at ECE Department, Galgotias College of Engineering & Technology in Greater Noida, Uttar Pradesh, was a resounding success! The event, conducted in a hybrid mode, brought together a global community of researchers, academics, and industry professionals. The conference provided a dynamic platform for sharing cutting-edge research, fostering collaborations, and advancing knowledge across multiple disciplines.

2025: International Conference on Computing, Communication, and Sustainable Energy Technologies (I3CSET 2025)

International Conference on Computing, Communication, and Sustainable Energy Technologies

I3CSET-2025 brought together top researchers, scientists, and engineers from across the world to share their latest studies and experiences. Hosted by Galgotias College, this major international event focused on the intersection of AI, networking, and green energy solutions. Sponsored by **Taylor & Francis Group**, the conference underscored its commitment to academic transparency and high editorial standards.
