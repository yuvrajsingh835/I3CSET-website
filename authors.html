Information for Authors

Guidelines and instructions for I3CSET-2026 presenters

Important Notice

If you are an author and/or presenter of an article at I3CSET-2026, please note the following guidelines to ensure a smooth conference experience.

  • All accepted and presented quality papers will be indexed by Scopus.
  • Acceptance of articles is made with the understanding that at least one author will attend the conference to deliver a presentation; severe disruption to a session is caused if an article is not presented. In the event that one of the article's authors cannot attend, the corresponding author should inform the Program Chair as soon as possible.
  • In order to submit the final version of the manuscript and abstract, at least one of the authors of the article must register for the conference to upload the final manuscript.
  • All necessary forms needed for submission of the final manuscript will be created by the submission website at the time of final submission.
  • Please use the standard article templates for formating.
  • The nominal page limit for all articles is five (5) pages; articles exceeding these limits are subject to a page over-length fee. Excess page charges are applicable per page (maximum 8 pages total).
  • Authors should bring their own notebooks/tablets/laptops/Pendrives for presentations. Projectors support HDMI / Type-C, but we recommend bringing your own adapters to ensure compatibility.
