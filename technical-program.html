Program Overview

The I3CSET-2026 Technical Program will feature a comprehensive lineup of keynote talks, tutorials, and technical sessions. The full schedule will be released following the finalization of the peer-review process.

Conference Tracks

Track 1: Communication Systems and 6G Technologies
Communication Protocols for 6G, Wireless and Mobile Communication Technologies, Communication Theory, Digital Communication, Satellite Communication, Telecommunication and Networking, Advanced Communication Systems, Green Communication, and Environmentally Sustainable Communication Technologies.
Track 2: Signal, Image, and Multimedia Processing
Digital Signal Processing Algorithms and Architectures, Signal and Image Processing Techniques, Image Analysis, Multimedia Processing, and Multimedia Engineering.
Track 3: Embedded Systems, IoT, and Cyber-Physical Networks
Internet of Things (IoT), Embedded Systems Design, Wireless Sensor Networks, Ad Hoc Networks, Robotics, Control Systems, and Control Theory.
Track 4: Antennas, Propagation, and Microwave Technologies
Antenna Design and Propagation, Microwave Communication Systems, Optical Communication, Optoelectronics, and Photonics.
Track 5: Cybersecurity, Blockchain, and Information Technologies
Cybersecurity and Blockchain Technologies, Network and Data Security, Information Theory and Coding, Data Communications, and Information Technology Applications.
Track 6: Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Data Science
Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning Algorithms, Pattern Analysis and Classification, Pattern Recognition, Image Processing, Big Data Analytics, Data Science, Optimization Techniques, Software Systems, Database Technologies, Bioinformatics, and Scientific Computing.
Track 7: Electronic Design, VLSI, and Digital Circuits
Electronic Circuits and Systems, Discrete Electronic Circuits, VLSI Design and Testing, Computer Architecture, and Digital Systems.
Track 8: Cloud, Quantum, and Advanced Computing Paradigms
Cloud and Edge Computing, Quantum Computing, Advanced Computing Architectures, Engineering Education, Engineering Management, and the Social Implications of Technology.
Track 9: Sustainable Computing, Smart Energy, and Green Technologies
Sustainable Computing, Green IT, Power and Energy Systems, Smart Grids, Renewable Energy Technologies, Disaster Management, and Humanitarian Technologies.
Track 10: Biomedical Systems, Bioelectronics, and Oceanic Engineering
Biomedical Engineering Systems, Bioelectronics, Biosensors, Marine Engineering, and Offshore and Oceanic Technologies.
Track 11: Miscellaneous and Interdisciplinary Topics
Emerging Interdisciplinary Applications, Multidisciplinary Research Integration, Technological Innovations for Society, Engineering Education and Management, Agricultural Applications, Disaster Mitigation Technologies, and Societal Impact of Emerging Technologies.
